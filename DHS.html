National Health Authority

National Health Authority (NHA) is the apex body responsible for implementing India’s flagship public health insurance/assurance scheme called “Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana” & has been entrusted with the role of designing strategy, building technological infrastructure and implementation of “Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission” to create a National Digital Health Eco-system. Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission

ABDM will connect the digital health solutions of hospitals across the country with each other. The Mission will not only make the processes of hospitals simplified but also will increase ease of living. The Digital Ecosystem will also enable a host of other facilities like Digital Consultation, medical practitioners can access patient's records throught their consent, etc. With ABDM, every record will be stored digitally. UP Medical Health & Family Welfare

The Department of Medical Health and Family Welfare, Uttar Pradesh, works on healthcare delivery, public health programs, family welfare initiatives, and medical education. The department has undertaken various initiatives like National Health Mission, and Ayushman Bharat Yojana to enhance healthcare accessibility and quality in the state.
